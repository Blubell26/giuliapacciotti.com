About me

I didn't wait until I had it
figured out.

A few years ago I packed up our lives and left. No safety net, no tidy plan. Just a deep knowing that the conventional version wasn't going to work for us.

Since then: unschooling my son, learning through life rather than a curriculum, building income that comes with us, natural healing, deep inner work and nervous system regulation. And writing honestly about what all of it actually looks like. Without the spiritual ego or fancy background. Wild Hearts Rising. The Freedom Letters. Keepers of the Wild Heart. Pieces of a life that keeps making itself up.

I'm not a guru at the top of a mountain looking back down. I'm still in it. That's the point.

The unconventional path isn't easier. But it's yours. So you'll find meaning in it. And there is a way to make it work financially.
— Giulia

A few things about this space

Keepers of the Wild Heart is the overarching brand for everything I do. It holds the parenting work, the inner work, the body healing and the income holistically together.

  • Several years of slow travel through Europe with my son
  • Unschooling as a philosophy and a practice to reconnect with our own guidance
  • Ten years without a doctor. A kid who doesn't get sick. Not luck. A way of living.
  • Building digital income without becoming someone else
  • Writing that tells the truth about what this actually looks like

Parenting / Unschooling

What if learning
looked like living?

We've been unschooling through Europe for years. It's not perfect. It's not always easy. But watching my son learn through actual life has changed what I think education even is.

  • Hands-on support for mums navigating unconventional education
  • Building programs for families who want to go their own way

Keepers of the Wild Heart is about finding ways to live an intentional life without becoming outsiders on a mountain in the process. Without limiting ourselves to an identity or a group.

It is rejoining the flow of things that moves us to where we need to be, or the version of us we have to become. Takes what works, leaves the rest. Use logic but also free up your heart. Keeps things simple. And stays open enough to challenge what everyone else takes for granted.
