WHR-Mastering the Inner Flame

For the sincere seeker who knows something needs to change and is ready to stop waiting for permission to begin.

Explore the guide

Keepers of the Wild Heart is about finding ways to live an intentional life without becoming outsiders in the process. Without limiting ourselves to an identity or a group.

It is rejoining the flow of things that moves us to where we need to be, or the version of us we have to become. Takes what works, leaves the rest. Use logic but also free up your heart. Keeps things simple. And stays open enough to challenge what everyone else takes for granted.
