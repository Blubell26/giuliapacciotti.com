          <h2 class="sec-h2">I didn't wait until I had it<br><em>figured out.</em></h2>
          <div class="sec-rule"></div>
          <p>A few years ago I packed up our lives and left. No safety net, no tidy plan. Just a deep knowing that the conventional version wasn't going to work for us.</p>
          <p>Since then: <strong>unschooling my son</strong>, learning through life rather than a curriculum, building income that comes with us, natural healing, deep inner work and nervous system regulation. And writing honestly about what all of it actually looks like. Without the spiritual ego or fancy background. Wild Hearts Rising. The Freedom Letters. Keepers of the Wild Heart. Pieces of a life that keeps making itself up.</p>
          <p>I'm not a guru at the top of a mountain looking back down. I'm still in it. That's the point.</p>
          <div class="about-pull">
            <blockquote>This path isn't easier. But it's yours to take. Dorothy had the power the whole time. She just didn’t know it, so she walked the whole road to Oz to be told what was already hers. I think about how much of what I’ve called an injustice in my own life was actually just me. Not someone taking my freedom. Me, never noticing it was sitting right there.</blockquote>
            <cite>— Giulia</cite>
